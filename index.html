Welcome to the website of our Hindu Shiva temple. Our temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva, one of the most revered deities in Hinduism.

Temple History

Our temple was built in the 12th century and is one of the oldest Shiva temples in the region. It is known for its beautiful architecture and intricate carvings.

Temple Timings

The temple is open from 6am to 9pm every day.
